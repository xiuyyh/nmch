# **App Name**: TapTrack Bar Operations

## Core Features:

- Sale Entry: Allow staff to quickly record individual drink and food sales, including quantity, price, and payment method.
- Inventory Tracking: View current stock levels for all bar items (liquors, beers, mixers, food) and update quantities manually or through sale deductions.
- Menu Item Configuration: Add, edit, or remove menu items with their prices, categories, and associated inventory components.
- Stock Request Generation: Generate a list of items to be restocked based on current inventory levels and predefined minimum stock thresholds.
- Basic Sales Report: View daily or weekly sales summaries to track performance of individual items and overall revenue.

## Style Guidelines:

- The app uses a sophisticated dark theme, appropriate for a bar setting. The background is a very dark, desaturated blue (#141920) for a modern, sleek feel.
- The primary accent color is a vibrant yet refined blue (#58A4ED), providing clarity and highlighting key actions and information.
- A bright, energetic purple (#CF80FF) serves as an accent for call-to-action elements, alerts, and to draw attention to critical insights without overwhelming the primary palette.
- Headlines use 'Space Grotesk', a proportional sans-serif, to convey a modern and efficient tone, suitable for displaying data and action titles.
- Body text uses 'Inter', a clear and neutral sans-serif, ensuring excellent readability for lists, reports, and detailed inventory information.
- Employ clean, minimalist line icons that are easily recognizable and reinforce the app's focus on efficiency and professionalism for bar operations.
- A modular, card-based layout prioritizes critical information for each operational area, allowing for intuitive navigation and quick data consumption across different device sizes commonly found in a bar environment.
- Subtle, functional animations will be used for state changes, loading indicators, and confirmations, enhancing the user experience without being distracting.
